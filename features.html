classes

.card.shoelace-card: Provides a styled card container with a dark theme for displaying content.

.card-title: Formats the title text inside a card with a white color and bold font.

.card-text: Styles the text content inside a card with a light gray color and a smaller font size.

.shoelace-heading: Creates a heading with a white color and bold font, suitable for titles.

.shoelace-paragraph: Formats paragraphs with a light gray color and a moderate font size for regular text.

.shoelace-image: Adds styling to images, including a border, shadow, and maximum width for better presentation.

.btn: Styles buttons with a gray background color, white text, and rounded corners for general use.

.shoelace-btn: Similar to .btn, it further enhances button styles for Shoelace-specific components.

.nav-link: Applies styling to navigation links with hover effects and appropriate color.

.navbar: Formats the navigation bar with a dark theme and a visible box shadow for a modern look.

.shoelace-list: Adds a visible box shadow to lists for a visually appealing layout.

.shoelace-quote: Formats quotes with a visible box shadow for a clean presentation.

.shoelace-form: Applies styling to forms, making form elements aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly.

.btn:hover: Gives a darker background color on button hover, enhancing the interactive experience.

.footer: Styles the footer with a dark theme and padding, providing a cohesive design for the page.

.shoelace-feature: For elements highlighting key features, it adds styling with a dark background and shadow.

.shoelace-feature-title: Formats feature titles with a white color and bold font for emphasis.

.shoelace-feature-description: Styles feature descriptions with a light gray color and moderate font size.
